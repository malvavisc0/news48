# Skill: Adapt parsing to article type

## Trigger
Conditional — active for non-standard article types.

## Rules
### Live blog / rolling updates
- Chronological concise recap
- Keep only major confirmed updates

### Opinion / analysis
- Separate facts from commentary tone
- Avoid presenting opinion as fact

### Listicle / feature
- Preserve structure, compress repetitive filler

### Breaking alert
- Prioritize who, what, where, when, impact
